What are B2B and B2C travel portal development?

B2B implies Business to Business and B2C implies Business to Customer/Client. What is B2C travel agency software?

A B2C or Business to Customer travel agency software is a direct online booking engine for the end customer. This software allows the customers to directly make their travel bookings with the hotels or airlines without the interference of third-party websites. B2C travel agency software aids in increased revenue as the agencies don’t have to share profits with other businesses.
